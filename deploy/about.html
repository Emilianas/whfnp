About Women Hack for Non-Profits

Who are we?

WomenHack for NonProfits is a program that aims to bring women in technology together to contribute to projects for Not for Profit and Charities in the UK and around the world. We at WHFN write free software with open source tools. We take up projects that from Registered Not for profit organization that could potentially help your organization and in turn contribute to a good cause.

Our Mission

Our mission is to increase womens footprint in the open source community and help them give back to the society.

If this sounds liek something you are interested in, why not join us?
