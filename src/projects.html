Fashion Revolution Wall

Django, Python, HTML, Sass, JavaScript, Heroku

Fashion Revolution raise awareness of the true cost of fashion and show that together we can create a more sustainable future for our fashion industry.

WHFNP volunteers built a Fashion revolution Wall Django app to help raise awareness during Fashion Revolution Week.The wall pulled in twitter images submissions via a given hashtag to create a submission wall.

Findacure

Wordpress

Findacure is a charity dedicated to building the fundamental diseases community to drive research and develop treatments.

WHFNP volunteers built a new page on Findacure's Wordpress website to display members of the organization's new membership scheme. The page was formatted to include each member's bio, logo, and link back to their website. Volunteers also created a database and the ability to store, update, and search membership data.

IPPR

Express, CartoDb, Leaflet, ejs, jQuery

Institute for Public Policy Research (IPPR) is an organisation with a mission to deliver independent, analytical, and critical research into social, political and economic issues.

The objective of our project with IPPR is to build a website to improve the transparency using open data. Our technology stack includes Node.js and Express on the backend, EmbeddedJs templates and jQuery on the front end, CartoDb's PostGIS database for data storage, data manipulation and analysis tools, and Leaflet for map visualisations.

Pimp My Cause

Django, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Heroku

Pimp My Cause connects marketers, researchers, and innovators who wish to volunteer to good causes in need of innovative solutions. The organisation has successfully matchmade over 1,300 charities and volunteers since its inception in 2011.

WHFNP volunteers are tackling a website rebuild project for this charity to modernise the technology stack and improve the site's user experience. Using Django, Sass and JavaScript, the end goal is to leave Pimp My Cause with a slick new website that is robust, sustainable, and capable of supporting the growth in its membership expected over the next few years.
