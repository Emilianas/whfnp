About Women Hack for Non-Profits

Who are we?

Women Hack for Non Profits is a program that brings passionate women in technology together to collaborate on projects for registered non profits and charities or individuals with a great cause, both in the UK and around the world. We write free, high quality software with open source tools and provide our partners support for all stages of a project cycle, from planning to design to deployment. For volunteers, we aim to provide a fulfilling environment where you can both contribute your skills and learn new ones through real-world practice, workshops and ongoing tech events.

Our Mission

We aim to enhance the skill set of Women in Tech to support their growth and increase their footprint in the open source community, using technology to help non profits and individuals with good causes. If this sounds like something you are passionate about, join us!
